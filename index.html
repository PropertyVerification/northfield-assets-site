FAQ

What happens each week?

We walk an agreed path, document consistent areas with timestamped photos, and flag exceptions only.

Where does the documentation live?

Photos are stored consistently in a shared folder structure so managers can reference current condition and trends.

Is this a replacement for inspections?

No. It fills the visibility gap between formal inspections and regional visits.

Do you manage repairs or vendors?

No. Your team stays in control. We provide evidence so issues can be addressed earlier.

How long is a walkthrough?

Typically 20 to 45 minutes depending on property size and the agreed walk path.

Who is this best for?

Regional and portfolio managers overseeing multiple assets, especially during scrutiny moments.
