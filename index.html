FAQ

What exactly do you do?

We perform routine, independent walkthroughs of agreed common areas and document conditions with timestamped photos.

Is this a replacement for inspections?

No. This fills the visibility gap between inspections and regional visits by creating consistent on-site documentation.

How do you handle issues you notice?

We flag exceptions with evidence to the designated contact. We do not manage repairs, vendors, or emergencies.

Where does the documentation live?

Photos are organized in a shared folder structure so managers can quickly reference current condition and trends over time.
